LD Capital Bridge to USA, LLC

LD Capital Bridge to USA provides immigration consulting and services to people who want to get a US Green Card for themselves and their dependent family through a US government program called EB-5. The EB-5 program is an Investment based Immigration program for USA permanent residency. It is the fastest way to get a USA Green Card. The beneficiaries are the Investor, their spouse, and unmarried children under the age of 21. You need to Invest $800,000 for 5 years in a New Commercial Enterprise, offered by the Regional Center. There is no English fluency, minimum education qualification or business experience criteria for the EB-5 Program. Just a legal source of the funds and no criminal background of the Investor is required.
